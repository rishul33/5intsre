Payroll Outsourcing Services

Apart from handling your business, you need to look after the payrolls. It includes employee salaries, tax calculations, accounts, leaves, payable days, etc. Though small or medium-sized firms can handle these strenuous tasks in-house, outsourcing them to experienced teams can give them an added advantage. The businesses can focus on their core forte and business development while a professional team handles the Payroll Management for them.

Talensetu offers expert payroll management across the country wherein the payroll responsibility will be completely handled by us.

Our payroll management services take care of your specific needs and offer assistance for diverse needs round-the-clock.

What We Offer?

  • Monthly Salaries
    Every organization needs a strong Payroll Management system, which will perform certain required tasks promptly and consistently such as payroll processing, salary slips, and generating statutory reports as and when needed. The salaries are given to the employees according to their job profile, rank, and seniority. Talensetu handles this for you with the help of a well-defined process. These regular activities are the base of periodic tax and government compliances.
  • Annual Activities
    Managing the recurring monthly activities flawlessly is highly necessary for the smooth running of your business. Our annual assistance takes care of all the activities like IT computation, handling Form 16 and Form 25 along with receipt and verification of declaration and proofs. We follow different checklists, which ensure timely and accurate completion of essential documentation and processes.
  • MIS and various regular Reports
    Maintaining regular MIS records and reports is mandatory for every business however it is time-consuming and tedious. Let us handle it for you where we will generate and maintain several needed reports as IT reports, bank advice reports, staff leave reports and the most important are pay registers of the organization.
The secret behind successful companies is they do their business efficiently. You can do that too. Our payroll services will make your job more efficient. We have our agency offices in Pune, Mumbai and, Bengaluru. Our Payroll Management team will assist you with their expertise and your business will reach new heights of success. The Talensetu team not only handles the growing and complex needs of your business with accuracy and speed but also maintains the highest confidentiality regarding all your data. Call us or write to us to know more about our offerings; we can offer you a customized service package suitable for your unique needs.
